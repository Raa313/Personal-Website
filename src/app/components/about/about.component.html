About Me

Hi! I'm Ryan Harmon, a recent Computer Science graduate from Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology with a minor in Economics.

I'm passionate about building efficient, scalable software solutions across the full stack. My experience ranges from developing full-stack booking platforms for government agencies to creating GPU-accelerated animation frameworks in Rust, and designing custom computer processors in Verilog.

During my time at Washoe County Government, I developed a full-stack booking platform that streamlined inmate intake processes and migrated their login system to Microsoft account synchronization with group-based security protocols. I've also worked extensively in data science and machine learning, utilizing tools like TensorFlow, Pandas, and Scikit-learn.

Beyond coding, I was an active member of the RoboMasters Robotics Team, where I design and program robot control frameworks in C++ and Python for international robot combat competitions. I've contributed to the successful design and deployment of 3 competition robots.
